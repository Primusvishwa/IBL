About Integrated Bioprocess Laboratory (IBL)

Mission: To innovate sustainable, biosurfactant-based solutions for global challenges.

Vision: Establish IBL as a leading academic and industrial brand in biosurfactants.

Focus Areas:

  • R&D in Rhamnolipids, Lipopeptides, and Sophorolipids.
  • High-purity enzyme-based patented technologies.
  • Large-scale and immobilized enzyme production capabilities.

Academic Foundation: Combining cutting-edge research with practical applications.

Scalability: From small-scale lab production to industrial-scale output.
